Panner node
Panner node
defines 
  • position, 
  • orientation
  • velocity 
of a sound.
context has a listener attribute 
that defines 
  • position
  • orientation
  • velocity 
of the listener. 
With these two things you can create directional sounds 
with doppler effects and 3D panning.
